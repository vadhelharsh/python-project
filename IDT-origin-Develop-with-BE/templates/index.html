Title: "Integrated Hospital Navigation and Patient Info Portal"

Description: This project aims to assist families during emergency hospital visits by combining hospital navigation with a patient information portal. In critical situations, patients are often brought in unconscious or without proper documentation, making it difficult to contact family members or keep them informed.
The system includes the following key features:
Hospital Navigation: A map-based tool helps family members find the correct hospital, especially when multiple hospitals have similar names nearby. This avoids confusion and saves precious time.
Emergency Contact Access: Hospital staff can view emergency contact details from sources like Aadhaar cards, college IDs, or mobile lock screen emergency info. This allows them to quickly inform the patient’s family about the situation.
Patient Information Portal: A web-based system where family members can access real-time updates about the patient’s condition, ward number, block, required funds, and other important details.
Staff Entry Panel: A private form used by a designated hospital staff member to upload patient information into the system. This includes patient ID, current condition, contact number (used as a unique ID), treatment details, and more.
Phone Number-Based Access: Family members can enter the phone number used by hospital staff to contact them, and the portal will display the patient’s info. This makes it easy to access updates without needing a login or ID card.
(Optional) Direct Link via SMS/WhatsApp: To make things even smoother, staff can send a direct link to the family via SMS or WhatsApp. Clicking the link takes them directly to the patient’s info page.
